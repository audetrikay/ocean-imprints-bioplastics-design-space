# Experimental Protocol


## Variables

- Glycerine: low (2g) / high (6g)  
- Water: freshwater / seawater  
- Method: immersion / evaporation  
- Time: short (6h) / long (3d)  
- Light: with / without  

---

## Steps

### 1. Film preparation
- Mix alginate + water  
- Add glycerine  
- Cast films
- Spray calcium lactate 
- Dry (48-56h)  
 

---

### 2. Exposure

**Immersion**
- Samples fully submerged during 6 hours, 2 days and 6 days 

**Evaporation**
- Samples sprayed, then dried  

---

### 3. Observation

At each time step:
- flexibility  
- deformation  
- texture  
- crystallization
