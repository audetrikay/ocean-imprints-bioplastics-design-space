# Alginate Bioplastic Recipe

## Formulations

- Low glycerine (rigid film):
  - Sodium alginate: 2–3 g  
  - Distilled water: 100 mL  
  - Glycerine: 2 g  

- High glycerine (flexible film):
  - Sodium alginate: 2–3 g  
  - Distilled water: 100 mL  
  - Glycerine: 6 g  

---

## Materials

- Sodium alginate powder  
- Distilled water  
- Glycerine  
- Calcium lactate  
- (Optional) food coloring  

---

## Equipment

- Beaker  
- Stirrer or blender  
- Precision scale  
- Flat casting surface (plate / mold)  
- Spray bottle  

---

## Procedure

### 1. Solution preparation

1. Pour 100 mL distilled water into a beaker  
2. Add sodium alginate progressively under continuous stirring  
3. Mix until fully dissolved (no visible particles)  
   - if needed, use a blender to improve homogeneity  
4. Add glycerine  
5. Mix until uniform  

---

### 2. Casting

1. Pour the solution onto a flat surface  
2. Spread evenly to obtain constant thickness  
3. Remove air bubbles (resting or light tapping)  
4. Leave to dry at room temperature (48-46 h)  

---

### 3. Crosslinking

1. Prepare calcium lactate solution:
   - 5–10 g/L in distilled water  
2. Spray uniformly onto dried films  
3. Wait 2–5 minutes  
4. Let dry again if needed  


- Film thickness:
  - thicker => slower drying, higher resistance  

- Calcium exposure:
  - increases rigidity through crosslinking
