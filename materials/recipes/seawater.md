# Artificial Seawater Recipe

## Composition (1 L)

- Sodium chloride (NaCl): 40 g  
- Magnesium sulfate (MgSO₄·7H₂O): 6 g  
- Calcium lactate: 5 g  
- Disodium hydrogen phosphate (Na₂HPO₄): 0.5 g  
- Distilled water: up to 1 L  

---

## Materials

- NaCl  
- MgSO₄·7H₂O  
- Calcium lactate  
- Na₂HPO₄  
- Distilled water  

---

## Equipment

- Beaker (≥1 L)  
- Stirrer  
- Scale  

---

## Procedure

1. Pour ~800 mL distilled water into a container  
2. Add salts one by one while stirring:
   - NaCl  
   - MgSO₄  
   - Calcium lactate  
   - Na₂HPO₄  
3. Stir until complete dissolution  
4. Add distilled water to reach 1 L  
5. Mix thoroughly
